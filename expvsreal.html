Campus

I had thought that Indian institute of Information Technology Ranchi is a college of the national importance hence its area would be quite large , but when I came to know that its permanent campus has not been built yet , I thought that its temporary campus must be running in some good campus. But when I came , I saw that first year classes were being held in small campus of Jharkhand University of Technology . And 2nd, third and fourth year classes are being held in another campus named JUPMI .
